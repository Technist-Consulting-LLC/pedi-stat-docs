PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Principles

'Pedi STAT' was created to enable physicians and other health care professionals to learn about managing critically ill pediatric patients. In order to achieve this objective, this app that provides educational materials about the management of critically ill children. So that health care professionals feel comfortable using our application, we work to ensure that the privacy and security of your personal information is our primary concern. The following Privacy Policy provides details regarding how this is done.


Information Collected


We collect information generated as you interact with our app (such as your mobile device type, IP address, operating system, date/time stamp, educational tools used and time spent using each tool).


Use of Information Collected


When you register on the 'Pedi STAT' app, the information you provide forms the basis of your personal profile. This information is stored by QxMD primarily to provide you with personalized content delivery and manage your account.


We do not collect or otherwise utilize your personal information.


Links


Within the Pedi STAT app there may be links to other websites. This Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected by the app itself and has no responsibility for external web sites and their privacy practices.


Changes to this Privacy Policy


We reserve the right to revise or update this Privacy Policy at any time. In the event that significant changes are made, we commit to advising all users at the time that any significant change is made. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by email, or by means of a notice on our home page or app.


If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement and other places we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it.


If you have any comments, questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please send a message to our privacy officer at talk@technistconsulting.com.
